FOCACCIA WITH CARAMELIZED ONIONS, CRANBERRIES AND BRIE

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetized topped with sweet caramelized onions,tart cranberries and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image Sally Vegas

FEATURE IN:
Fresh cramberry recipes that go beyond sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • cups (550g) all purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspons istant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 10 ounces brie,cut into cubes
  • 11/2 cups fresh o frozen cramberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium size mixing bowl, or in a stand mixed with a paddle attachment, mix toghether all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully aborbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
